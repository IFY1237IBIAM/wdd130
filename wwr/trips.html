Ibiams Rafting Company

Rapids Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating journey through the heart of the wilderness. Our Rapids Adventure trip takes you through challenging Class IV and V rapids, offering an adrenaline rush like no other. Navigate steep drops, twisting currents, and stunning landscapes. This trip is for experienced rafters looking for the ultimate thrill.

Scenic Explorer

For those who prefer a mix of excitement and serenity, our Scenic Explorer trip is the perfect choice. Enjoy a day on the river with gentle Class II and III rapids, offering a relaxed and family-friendly experience. As you float down the river, take in the breathtaking scenery and make lasting memories with your loved ones.

Sunset Expedition

Experience the magic of rafting as the sun sets over the water. Our Sunset Expedition offers a unique adventure with a twilight twist. Paddle through calm waters and watch the sky transform with vibrant colors. This trip is ideal for couples and groups seeking a romantic and tranquil rafting experience.
